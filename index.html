About

Come-Million is a giant colorful chameleon that shines beautifully in million colors. Her skin is made of a scales that carry thousands of little lights. As a social animal, the colors change in reaction to the explorers inside it, sensed through its pumping heart.

Mission

We are about to create something exciting that is greater than the sum of our parts - fusing our skills together to make something that we could not otherwise achieve. With this spirit of connection we will start a journey and bring to life our mindfruit - “Come-Million!”
